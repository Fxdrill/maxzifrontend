# Images Folder

## Instructions:
1. Add your product images here
2. Recommended size: 500x500 pixels (square)
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP

## Logo:
- Name your logo file: `logo.png` or `logo.svg`
- Recommended size: 200x50 pixels (horizontal logo)

## Product Images:
- Place product images in subfolders by category
- Or simply add them here with descriptive names

## Note:
All images in this folder will be accessible at `/images/filename.extension`
